Fried Rice

Description

This is one of my go-to's meals when I'm out of time and want something delicious. It's not complicated and it only takes 10 minutes. The perfect meal.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Garlic
  • Green Onion
  • Eggs
  • Meat
  • Carrot
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice or microwave during 3 minutes left-over rice.
  2. Chop garlic, green onion and carrot into small bite-size pieces.
  3. Beat the eggs and add some salt
  4. Add oil to the pan and cook the eggs until done. Then, take them off into a plate
  5. Add oil to the pan and add garlic, green onion and carrot, cook for a couple of minutes.
  6. Add rice, cook for a while, and add soy sauce and some salt.
  7. Done!
